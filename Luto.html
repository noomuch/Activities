Dim Sum

  1. 10 oz Ground pork
  2. 1/2 Brown onion
  3. 1/2 tsp Ginger, root
  4. 12 Green peas
  5. 2 Lettuce or nappa cabbage
  6. 3 Shiitake mushroom, dried
  7. 1 Japanese yellow mustard
  8. 1 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce
  9. 3 tbsp Cornstarch
  10. 1 Pepper
  11. 1/4 tsp Salt
  12. 1/2 tsp Sugar
  13. 1 tbsp Rice vinegar
  14. 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  15. 12 Shumai or wonton wrappers
  1. Make the Dough
    - Mix flour salt and oil, then add water til it gradually becomes dough.
  2. Kneed and let it rest:
    - In a clean surface knead until smooth and elastic and cover with a damp cloth to prevent drying.
  3. Prepare the filling:
    - Mix the ground pork with soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, scallions, and other desired seasonings..
  4. Vegetables (optional):
    - Add finely chopped vegetables like carrots, cabbage, or water chestnuts for added texture.
  5. Cook the Dim Sum:
    - Line a steamer basket with parchment paper. Place the wrapped dumplings in the steamer basket, ensuring they are not touching. Steam for 8-10 minutes, or until cooked through.
